About Me

My name is Veronica Lozada, I'm 22 years old. I'm from Caracas, Venezuela and I recently moved to Atlanta looking for personal and professional growth and to have new experiences in life. I just graduated from Industrial Engineering at the Universidad Simon Bolivar located in Caracas. I had the opportunity to live in Barcelona for a year as an exchange student and has been one of the best experiences in my life, getting to know people from all over the world and to experience a different culture than mine. I love to travel and meet new places everytime I can.
